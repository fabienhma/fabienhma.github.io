Hi! My name is Fabien Ma.

I am a Hong Kong-born, Taipei- and Shanghai-raised Applied Mathematics and Computer Science student at Brown University, currently halfway through my third year. Prior to starting at Brown, though, I spent a year in France (Vichy, then Paris), where I became fluent in French, and also spent 6 months at Le Cordon Bleu, learning the art of French cuisine.

I'm interested in all things tech, and this website is an attempt at showcasing some of what I know. It's hosted on GitHub pages so a lot of apps that use server-side code can't really be hosted here, but you can check out my github page for some of the stuff that I've done. This page was developed with the help of Bootstrap, specifically the Cover template, so big thanks to them and especially @mdo.

Anyway, besides my interested in software engineering and learning more about Computer Science and how to write better, cleaner code, I'm also very interested in cooking and fitness.

I really think that, especially with the explosion of things like Crossfit and the subsequent movement of people into sports like powerlifting and weightlifting as well as wearable technology, that there is a lot of room for growth at the intersection of fitness and technology – we can use technology not only to gain metrics about the way that we live and how to improve them on a day-to-day scale, but technology can even allow us to write better programming - check out MyStrengthBook – or figure out where our muscular imbalances lie – EuMotus comes to mind.
